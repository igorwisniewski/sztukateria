ABOUT MY WORK A FEW WORDS

I would like to introduce you to my original idea of beautifying the interior of a room, which will interest you. The idea was born during the renovation of my own apartment. When I was browsing the Internet, I was looking for inspiration to beautify my room and I found a photo showing a wall sticker in the form of a tree. This made me curious

The tree is made of decorative plaster on a polystyrene base. The root, trunk, branches and fence are made of the same material as the leaves. The leaves are painted in several shades of green.

The whole, including the frames and the fence, is illuminated as you can see in the photos. The frames are removable with the possibility of replacing the photo. Not included: magnets on the frames and decorations such as a wooden well, a wooden windmill and a wooden bridge and flower baskets.

I direct my offer to people who appreciate the unique charm of the interior. Having such stucco on the wall you can boast to your family, friends and relatives. My works are admired by all who view them.
